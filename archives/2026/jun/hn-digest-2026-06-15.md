## 12. [Openrouter Fusion API](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48537641) (↑157, 💬61)
**Article:** OpenRouter's Fusion API turns a prompt into a multi-model deliberation where a panel of expert models analyzes the prompt in parallel, then a judge model synthesizes their responses into a structured analysis covering consensus, contradictions, and blind spots. Users can choose between a "Quality" preset with expensive frontier models or a "Budget" preset with cheaper ones, and pricing is the sum of all underlying model calls. The feature is designed for research, expert critique, and situations where the cost of being wrong outweighs paying for multiple completions.

**Comments:** Many commenters report building similar systems independently, with mixed results — some found that asking one model to judge another's response doesn't produce better answers, just more noise. A commenter's benchmark showed Fusion was 7x slower and 4x the cost of calling a single frontier model. The consensus is that this technique (essentially ensemble/test-time compute scaling) can improve results for specific use cases like research and planning, but is too expensive and slow for everyday use, and should be reserved for high-stakes tasks.
