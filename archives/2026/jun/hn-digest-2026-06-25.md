**Article:** This preprint argues that the "lottery ticket hypothesis" (that success in overparameterized networks comes from finding a lucky well-initialized subnetwork) is misleading, because winning tickets can fail if the rest of the network is perturbed. **Comments:** The few commenters generally agree with the paper’s critique of the lottery ticket hypothesis.
